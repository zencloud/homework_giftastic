About Giphtastic:

Giphtastic allows you to organize your Giphys by category and bookmark your favorite ones for quick reference!

Usage:

Tags:

  • Click a tag to show Giphys from that category. Keep clicking for more!
  • Add a new tag to customize your list.
  • Right click any tag to remove it from your list.

Giphys:

  • Click loaded Giphys to see the gif play.
  • Hover over a Giphy and click the heart to add to your favorites!
  • You can remove favorited Giphies from your favorites panel.
